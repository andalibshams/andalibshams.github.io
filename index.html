Hello, I am Andalib a Ph.D. student and research assistant in the Department of Civil Engineering at Iowa State University (ISU) under the supervision of Christopher M. Day. Prior to joining ISU, I did my MS in Civil Engineering from University of Wyoming in 2018. I worked there as a graduate assistant and supervised by Milan Zlatkovic . I did my Bachelors in Urban and Regional Planning from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) in 2015. My current research is in vehicle trajectory based adaptive traffic signal control.

Some of the topics I'm interested in are: Adaptive Traffic Signal Control, Traffic Modeling and Simulation, Traffic Operations.

Feel free to drop me an email if you want to talk about any of these!

Research
Traffic Signal Control Previously worked on assessing impacts of sensing ranges on advanced adaptive traffic signal control algorithms. Currently working on vehicle trajectory based adaptive traffic signal control. Also, working on offline offset optimization.

Traffic Modeling and Simulation I have been working on traffic micro-simulation for more than 4 years. I am mostly using VISSIM and extensively use VISSIM COM and API to implement our ideas. Currently working on developing a signal controller which will be helpful in visualizing our ideas.

Traffic Operations Interested in other fields of traffic operations, especially on traffic impact assessment. Previously worked on assessing impacts of bus rapid transit and innovative intersections like Continuous Flow Intersection and Diverging Diamond Interchange.
