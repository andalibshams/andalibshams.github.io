Hello, I am Andalib a Ph.D. student and research assistant in the Department of Civil Engineering at Iowa State University (ISU) under the supervision of Christopher M. Day. Prior to joining ISU, I did my MS in Civil Engineering from University of Wyoming in 2018. I worked there as a graduate assistant and supervised by Milan Zlatkovic . I did my Bachelors in Urban and Regional Planning from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) in 2015. My current research is in vehicle trajectory based adaptive traffic signal control.

Some of the topics I'm interested in are: Adaptive Traffic Signal Control, Traffic Modeling and Simulation, Traffic Operations.

Feel free to drop me an email if you want to talk about any of these!
